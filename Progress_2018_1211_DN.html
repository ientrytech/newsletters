Low-Code Platforms: What Developers Think and Why

The demand for applications in today’s business world has skyrocketed. Companies across the globe are now trying to figure out the best way to accelerate app delivery to keep up with this demand.

No and low-code platforms are being touted as the solution to this challenge, enabling "citizen developers" to build functional apps without having to write code. But are they too good to be true—can they actually help businesses quickly deliver the apps they need to succeed?

We polled more than 5,000 developers to find out what they think, asking questions like:

  • What would help you deliver apps faster?
  • How do you feel about platforms that promise to deliver apps without coding?
  • What concerns do you have about low-code platforms?
