Don't Let Images and Videos
Slow Down Your Site
Learn best practices for optimizing page load speed

• • •
It's been proven that visual media improves user experience, which results in higher engagement and better conversions. But large product images and video content files can also impact page load times, deflate your search rankings and turn visitors away if pages take too long to load.

This whitepaper offers tips and best practices to help you reconcile the "need for speed" with the need to provide an engaging site experience to your visitors and potential customers.
