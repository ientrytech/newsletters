Report: Consumers' Digital Demands

As consumer online transactions increase during COVID-19, fraudulent activity has also grown. Merchants are feeling pressure to keep up with the influx while building consumer trust through a secure digital experience. The loss of trust can negatively impact customer loyalty and encourage customers to shop with competitors.

Research firm Vanson Bourne partnered with Ekata to produce a new report, “Infinite Want: Consumers Demand Speed and Security in the Digital Experience.” More than 7,000 consumers across North America and Europe shared their digital experience expectations, how they respond when their needs are not met, and their perspectives on company responsibility around fraud and security.

According to the report, 92% of consumers demand trust, security, and data privacy, while also expecting digital transactions to be fast and frictionless.

The unprecedented COVID-19 crisis has left many businesses without a playbook. It’s more important than ever to understand your customers' needs, from the moment they open an account to the moment they check out.
